Mental Health Assessment

About this Assessment

This is a Mental Health Quality of Life (MHQoL-7D) assessment that helps measure your mental well-being across 7 dimensions. Your responses are confidential and will help track your progress over time.

Instructions: Please answer all 7 questions honestly based on how you've been feeling over the past week. There are no right or wrong answers.

Question 1 of 7

1. How would you rate your overall mood today?

2. How would you rate your anxiety levels today?

3. How would you rate your ability to cope with stress today?

4. How would you rate your energy levels today?

5. How would you rate your sleep quality last night?

6. How would you rate your social connections today?

7. How would you rate your overall sense of well-being today?
